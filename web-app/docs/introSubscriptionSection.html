Subscriptions

Perhaps the most powerful and important feature in Quoddy, subscriptions allow Users to register interest in various kinds of content from both within their organization, and from outside the organiation, and then have that content delivered straight to their Activity Stream.

Quoddy currently supports four types of Subscriptions:

  • Activiti User Tasks
  • Business Events
  • Calendar Feeds
  • RSS Feed Subscription

For more information on configuring Subscriptions please see the Subscriptions chapter.
